APS values

As part of our on-going development we are always interested to hear from professionals with varying ranges of experience and with the commitment and integrity to be able to respond to the challenges that new business brings for APS.

The values that shape APS Group are fundamental to our success – they are about what we stand for as an organisation and how we all behave. They frame a role for our business and give us a purpose beyond profit.

Integrity means ‘making a promise – keeping a promise’

Be responsible with resources and use them more efficiently and wisely. Always be mindful of our environment. Above all we must be genuine in the way we work and be relied upon to get the job done simply, accurately and on time.

Advanced thinking means ‘if there’s a better way? – let’s find it’

Make sure we remain competitive, dynamic and innovative by continuing to develop our capability to introduce new services. Make communications easier, embrace and develop new technology and make sure we give ourselves and our customers a competitive edge.

Team working means ‘none of us is as smart as all of us’

Strengthen our existing relationships and help build new ones by working as a team at all levels. Share resources and understanding so that when the customer succeeds, so do we. Encourage active listening and open communication. Gain commitment from customers to help achieve common goals.

Customer value means ‘we’re worth it’

Ask lots of questions and question lots of things in order that we can think differently. Look ahead and focus on what more can be achieved. We should not be afraid of change
and should consistently strive to improve things. Use our knowledge, practical know-how
and insight to make our customers’ brands better.
